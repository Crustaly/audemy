Technology

Conversational Interface Game Research

Audemy's games were optimized through a novel and rigourous testing methodology. We have researched and published studies about conversational interfaces at prestigous international research venues such as the Association for Computing Machinery's International Conference on Multimodal Interaction '23, where we presented our research to scholars in the field in Paris, France.

Google Cloud Natural Language Processing

Google Cloud's Natural language processing (NLP) uses machine learning to convert speech into text. With natural language processing applications, Audemy can communicate with users through a puerly audio-based interface.

Screen Reader Compatability

Audemy's game interface is created with a simplistic design so that blind and visually impaired students can easily navigate the different availible games with only a keyboard. Users use arrow keys to listen to game options and select games, and can answer questions through other keystrokes.
