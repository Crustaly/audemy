Initiatives

In addition to our game, Audemy is commited to inspiring change and education for blind and visually impaired students through other events and projects.

KatyYouthHacks

A hackathon focused on accessibility and social inequality created to inspire innovation in for people with disabilities. Through the hackathon, 500+ participants learned about project development, business pitching, and accessibility for the chance to win $70,000+ worth of prizes from corporate resources and sponosorships.

AppleLoo Educational Videos for Kids

Apple Loo Education is a social media brand created by Audemy to teach young students about educational topics. Apple Loo's videos series about animal fun facts has reached over 150,000+ students on YouTube.

Coding Accessibility Workshops

Audemy regularly hosts workshops focused on informing blind and visually impaired people about accessible resources to learn how to code.

Intro to Audemy Events

We host events to introduce our games to students through schools for the blind.
