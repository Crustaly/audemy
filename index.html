about

At Audemy, we aim to create an accessible platform for blind and visually impaired students to learn. Join us in our mission to transform education for all.

Audemy is committed to leveraging conversational interfaces to create a more inclusive educational landscape. By providing accessible and empowering learning experiences, we strive to help break down barriers and ensure that everyone has the opportunity to thrive academically.

how audemy works

Audemy utilizes natural language processing to enable blind and visually impaired individuals to learn through interactive voice-based interactions.

Audemy employs advanced natural language processing (NLP) technology to recognize and process spoken commands and questions. This streamlined process ensures efficient access to educational content, empowering users to learn effectively and independently.
